Minesweeper

Author: Phillip Lavergne

Click the image to download game.

Object of the game: determine where all 9 mines are without clicking on a square that contains one. The squares that contain a number indicate how many clickable squares within its radius contain a mine. Watch the time! When it hits zero, the game is over.

Snake Game

Author: Shukria Gulzar

Click the image to download game.

In the game, the snake is trying to eat an object (Food). The player controls the snake by roaming around, using the direction bottoms (right, left, up, and down keys) to play the game and make the snake eat the food object. The player must avoid the snake from hitting its own tail or the walls; if the snake does, then the game will be over and for playing again they need to start again.

Author: Alyx Stockton

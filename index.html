Minesweeper

Author: Phillip Lavergne

Click the image to download game.

Object of the game: determine where all 9 mines are without clicking on a square that contains one. The squares that contain a number indicate how many clickable squares within its radius contain a mine. Watch the time! When it hits zero, the game is over.

Snake Game

Author: Shukria Gulzar

Click the image to download game.

In the game, the snake is trying to eat an object (Food). The player controls the snake by roaming around, using the direction bottoms (right, left, up, and down keys) to play the game and make the snake eat the food object. The player must avoid the snake from hitting its own tail or the walls; if the snake does, then the game will be over and for playing again they need to start again.

Flappy Bird

Author: Aleksander Gomez

Click the image to download game.

In Flappy bird press space to 'fly' through as many pipes as you can. Pipes spawn randomly so you have to try and adjust the flight of the bird by pressing space to get through as many pipes as you can! 

Piano

Author: James Elves

Click the image to download game.

Test your rhythm with this piano game. Try to improve your score as you hit the notes! Press the A/S/D/F keys as the notes come down to raise the score! Aim to beat your friends!

Tetris

Author: Alyx Stockton

Click the image to download game.

Carefully place pieces as they fall from the top of the screen. Try and conform them to other pieces. If a horizontal line is completed, the line will break and your score will increase! The longer the game goes on, the faster the pieces will fall. Good luck! (Please note that the source code for this program was originally provided by Officer Jack Traven at Level up. My program is a modified version of that provided source code)
